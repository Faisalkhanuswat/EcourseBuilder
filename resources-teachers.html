Resources for Educators

E-Course Builder was created for professors to have the tools they need to develop the next generation of learners. Our advanced learning system is more than just an LMS – it is a comprehensive education management system equipped with everything needed to fuel success. Our LMS is equipped with our connective suite of software including LMS, whiteboard, video conferencing, documenting editing tool, presentation software, video creation tool, drive, and so much more.

Working Closely with Professors

Our team at E-Course Builder recognizes that real change comes from talking directly with our users. We spend significant time, energy, and effort connecting with professors and openly asking for their suggestions. We love hearing from professors and getting their input on what can be improved, what is working, what additional functionalities they may require, and always staying in the know of professor activity.
E-Course Builder works hard to provide around the clock customer service. Anytime a professor has a question, has an issue, needs help, our team is here to answer your questions promptly and it is our aim to get it right the first time.

Helping Professors Empower the Next Generation

Improving student performance is no easy feat. It requires monitoring every aspect of the performance of students and using that information to create courses that help the student learn better. E-Course Builder is a one-of-a-kind education management system that helps professors attain better student performance outcomes.
These performance measures help the professor see how well each student is performing in the class. When a student falls behind or doesn’t perform well, the system will alert the professor and student of what they are not doing correctly or have not completed through sending an email or alert to the student and teacher about the student’s circumstances such as not meeting minimum grade requirements, not enough logins, haven’t taken midterm/final, or are performing behind requirements. This is done automatically so all parties are aware. This keeps students accountable.

Tracking Student Performance

Improving student performance is central to why professors teach. Our proprietary technology allows professors to track all student performance measures and use that data to track student weaknesses. By addressing weaknesses with students, professors can positively influence student performance.
Professors can track grades by various measures including quiz and exam scores, time used to read course content, time spent on notes, number of student logins, and other indicators that determine how well a student is performing.

Automated Student Tracking

Our learning dashboard automatically tracks the following student patterns that bring about accountability:

  • If the student is falling behind
  • What are they falling behind in
  • How far behind have then fallen
  • Alert professor and student when a student is well below class average
  • Alert professor and student what the student is behind in
