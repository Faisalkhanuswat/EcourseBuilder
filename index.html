About US

The central goal of E-Course Builder is to empower professors to deliver their course content in the best manner possible and to improve student performance. E-Course Builder is a next generation learning management software that allows professors to fully customize their classes to the exact specifications they require to teach in the most effective manner. Our cutting-edge technology is a product of its own, with the ability for professors and students to customize E-books and course content, create digital flashcards, collaborate, make notes, and connect on our video conferencing technology.
