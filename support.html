Support

E Course Builder believes in live customer service. In a world where customer service has seemingly become forgotten, we are a company built on the idea of customer support. Our company bridges technology with live customer support 7 days a week.

Implementation

E-Course Builder is a full suite of educational technology products created to help teachers customize courses, maximize student performance, and give students and professors the opportunity to build a better future. Our team is here to help universities, their staffs, including professors and administrators, the ability to implement all core functionalities. E Course Builder is the next generation learning software system, and our team is here to help you at every turn of the corner.

Feedback

Our close work with professors, students, administrators, bookstore staff, Department chairs, and others in the university system has helped us gain the valuable insight into designing, developing, iterating, and constantly improving our education technology product line. E-Course Builder views all users as partners and not just clients. As partners in your pursuit of excellence, we must understand your university’s needs to create the next generation learning management educational system ever created.

Implementing Feedback

Our company mission statement includes one crucial component, which is to listen to all teacher, administrator, and student, requests, feedback, changes, and updates. We value the input of our users and recognize that the perspective, wants and requirements of our users is what we are here to serve. Feedback is at the heart of what we do.

Never Stopping

Education is a human right and resource that can never be taken away from an individual. Once that knowledge is learned it can never be taken away. E-Course Builder takes this right seriously and we do everything in our collective power to give professors the tools they need to empower the next generation of students. Our mission statement is paramount to our culture at E-Course Builder. To meet your expectations in a constantly evolving world, we are committed to always improving.
